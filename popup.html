Understanding Your Digital Ingredients

🛢️

Grease (Doomscrolling)

Endless scrolling creates "grease" in your digital diet. Too much makes everything feel heavy and unsatisfying.

🍭

Sugar (Short-form Content)

Quick dopamine hits from reels and shorts. Sweet in small doses, but too much causes crashes.

🧂

Salt (Tab Switching)

Rapid context switching adds "salt" - enhances focus in moderation, overwhelming when overdone.

🥬

Greens (Focused Reading)

Deep, focused content consumption. The healthy foundation of a balanced digital diet.

💧

Water (Mindful Breaks)

Conscious pauses and reflections. Essential for processing and digesting your digital experiences.
